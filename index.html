FAQs

  • What types of water filtration systems do you offer?

    We offer a variety of water filtration systems including under-sink filters, whole-house filtration systems, and countertop models. Each is designed to remove specific contaminants like chlorine, lead, and bacteria to improve taste and safety.

  • How often should I replace my filter cartridges?

    The lifespan of filter cartridges depends on the water quality and the amount of use, but generally, we recommend replacing them every 6 months to ensure optimal performance and water quality.

  • What are the benefits of using a water filtration system?

    Using a water filtration system improves the taste, odor, and clarity of your water. It also removes harmful contaminants and reduces the risk of consuming hazardous chemicals, providing you with safer and healthier drinking water.

  • Can your systems handle well water?

    Yes, our filtration systems are designed to handle well water. They can effectively remove common contaminants found in well water such as iron, manganese, and sediment, ensuring your water is clean and safe to use.

  • Do you provide installation services for filtration systems?

    Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our water filtration systems. Our experienced technicians ensure your system is installed correctly for optimal performance and compliance with all local plumbing codes.
